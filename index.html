A bit about me

I'm Abhi — I build things for the internet.

I'm a UK-based Shopify developer and automation architect with two years of hands-on experience building for e-commerce brands. I specialise in OS 2.0 theme development, custom Liquid sections, and designing backend automation systems that connect Shopify to the rest of a brand's tech stack.
